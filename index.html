🔐 Cyber Safety Tips

Stay protected online with these essential guidelines

🔗

Check URLs Carefully

Always verify the URL before clicking. Look for misspellings, extra characters, and suspicious domains that imitate real websites.

🔒

Look for HTTPS

Ensure websites use HTTPS encryption before entering any sensitive information like passwords or credit card details.

🔑

Strong Passwords

Use unique, complex passwords for each account. Mix uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters. Consider a password manager.

📱

Enable 2FA

Two-factor authentication adds an extra layer of security. Even if your password is compromised, your account stays protected.

🚫

Never Share OTPs

No legitimate company will ever ask for your OTP, PIN, or password via email, phone, or SMS. Report such attempts immediately.

📧

Verify Email Senders

Check email sender addresses carefully. Phishers use look-alike domains like "goog1e.com" or "amaz0n.com" to trick you.
